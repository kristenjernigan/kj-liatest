| Category | Topic | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| **Certification** | Program Purpose | The Digital Services Credential (DITAP), formerly FAC-C-DS, trains and develops professionals in digital service acquisition. |
|  | Program Structure | The program uses a cohort-based learning approach and is structured in modules and sprints. |
|  | Training Delivery | Training is delivered using a multi-modal approach supported by a learning management system. |
|  | Assessment & Evaluation | Participants are evaluated through pre- and post-course assessments, participation, mastery of content, and a live digital assignment. |
|  | Core Competencies | The curriculum covers understanding digital services, effective buying techniques, contract administration, and leading change in digital IT acquisition. |
|  | Learning Outcomes | The program emphasizes practical skill development through real-world application and problem-solving. |
| **Requirement** | Eligibility | FAC-C (Professional) holders with at least two years of experience and assigned to acquisitions consisting primarily of digital services over FAR 13.500(c) thresholds are required to attend the DITAP training program. |
| **Recommended** | Suggested Participants | FAC-COR Level II/III and FAC-P/PM Level II/III holders with at least two years of experience and assigned to acquisitions consisting primarily of digital services over FAR 13.500(c) thresholds are encouraged to attend DITAP training to create a cohesive team that fully understands how to buy digital services. |
